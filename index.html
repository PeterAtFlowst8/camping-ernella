The Philosophy

Preserved for generations.

Presided over by its owner, now in his 80s, Ernella remains a testament to a simpler time. This is not a holiday park; it is a quiet sanctuary maintained with deep respect for the land.

We invite those seeking true peace—away from the noise of digital notifications and modern rush—to pitch a tent by the water. There is no Wi-Fi here, and the cell service is faint, leaving you free to listen to the Tavignano river.

The Location

Hidden in the valley.

Located on the Route d'Ernella in Giuncaggio, our campsite is a refuge from the world. A single-track road leads you away from the coastline into the deep heart of the Tavignano valley.
